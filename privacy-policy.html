Your Privacy Matters to Us

Please review this Privacy Policy carefully to confirm your agreement with the data processing practices outlined by Internet Bundle Cable before proceeding. This document, along with the information provided herein, constitutes the entire agreement between you, the customer, and Internet Bundle Cable, your authorized local internet, cable, phone, and home security provider. By using our services, you acknowledge and commit to adhering to the policies outlined below.

As an authorized service provider, Internet Bundle Cable does not require disclaimers regarding affiliations with legitimate servers. We reserve the right to reject, modify, or remove any customer IDs, passwords, or PINs deemed inappropriate or insecure. Customers are required to promptly notify us of any changes to their personal or account information to ensure uninterrupted service. Failure to update information may result in service disruptions or account access issues.

2. Service Continuity and Maintenance

Internet Bundle Cable strives to provide reliable high-speed internet, cable TV, wireless phone, and home security services. However, service interruptions or reductions may occur due to necessary equipment upgrades, relocations, repairs, or other essential maintenance activities. Whenever possible, we will provide advance notice of planned service disruptions through email, our website, or other communication channels. We appreciate your understanding as we work to maintain and improve our network infrastructure.

3. Responsible Use of Services

Customers are expected to use the information, tools, and services provided through our cable, internet, phone, or payment platforms responsibly. This includes adhering to acceptable use policies, avoiding unauthorized access, and ensuring that account credentials are kept secure. By agreeing to this Privacy Policy, you commit to using our services in a manner that complies with all applicable laws and regulations.

4. Data Security

Your confidential information is securely protected and accessed only when necessary to perform tasks such as account management, billing, or service provisioning. Only authorized personnel with a legitimate need-to-know can access your personal data within our secure database. We employ industry-standard encryption, access controls, and monitoring to ensure the security and reliability of your information. Our commitment to data security is designed to protect your privacy and maintain your trust.

5. Information Disclosure

We may disclose your personal information if required by law, including in response to legal processes such as court orders, subpoenas, or other governmental requests. Additionally, we may share your information to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our customers or the public. Such disclosures will be made in accordance with applicable laws and regulations, and we will strive to limit the scope of any shared information to what is strictly necessary.

Internet Bundle Cable collects personal information such as your name, address, contact details, payment information, and service preferences to provide and manage our services. This data may be used for purposes including:

Your continued use of our services after such updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
